Our Story

Founded in 1898, Goodyear has been at the forefront of tyre innovation for over a century, driving advancements in safety, performance, and sustainability worldwide. Goodyear completes 100 years in India in 2022. It has two plants, one each in Ballabgarh and Aurangabad. In the passenger car segment, Goodyear supplies tyres to many of the leading OEMs in India and has been a pioneer in introducing tubeless radial tyres in the market. In the farm segment, Goodyear is a leading brand that supplies to all major tractor companies.
